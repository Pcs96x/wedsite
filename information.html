INFORMATION

INVITES ONLY

Please note, only people that have been given the link to the website are able to attend both, Church and Reception. However, we may be able to accomadate a 'plus one' if enough time is given and we do not exceed our guest numbers.

PHOTOS

Whilst we do not mind you taking photos at both venues, please respect the bride and groom and not post anything on social media before we do. There will also be a dedicated time slot for photos after the wedding.

Parking

As you may have read in the church description, it is uniquely placed in the middle of a roundabout. This means that you can not park directly outside the church. However, on each of the four exits, one side of the road allows you to park all day and there is also a carpark down Scarbough Avenue which is, roughly a 3 minute walk. Please allow time to get parked and walk to the church. The Reception has its own priviate carpark.

ATTIRE

The attire for the church is 'smart formal' however with drinks flowing and the music blasting, we appreciate you wil want to be comfy. So join the bride in bringing your slippers so nothing stops those dance moves!

CHILDREN

Having three gorgeous children between us, means we are welcoming children to both venues. We are having a disco, a bouncy castle and a secure private garden at the Reception. But we kindly ask you that children are not left unattended.

Staying over

If you are choosing to stay over, The Vine Hotel has kindly allocated a select number of rooms for our guests and honoured them a 15% discount if they book for wedding date. There also quite a few cheap B&B's located within a short walk from the reception, as well as a Premier Inn on the sea front. Feel free to message one of us if you would like any recommendations.
