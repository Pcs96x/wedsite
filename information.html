INFORMATION

Times

The ceremony at St. Matthews Church is due to start at 1pm. We are kindly asking that you arrive at the church and are seated for 12:45pm, in order for timings to be adhered to. When the ceremony is finished please make your way straight to the reception and grab yourself a drink!

RSVP

Please RSVP no later than the 14th of October. Due to having to confirm numbers with the venue, if we have not received your RSVP by then, unfortunately we will take this as you are unable to attend. Please head over to our RSVP page to either confirm or decline your invitation.

INVITES ONLY

Although we would like to be able to accommodate "plus ones" due to venue capacity, only guests receiving this invitation will be able to attend both the church and the reception. You will be notified if we have allocated you a plus one.

PHOTOS

Whilst we do not mind you taking photos, please respect the bride and groom and not post anything on social media before we do. Also we kindly ask that you are mindful of the paid photographer. Please could you send any photos or videos to: phillandgemmacoleman@gmail.com



Parking

As you may have read in the church description, it is uniquely placed in the middle of a roundabout. This means that you can not park directly outside the church. However, on each of the four exits, one side of the road allows you to park all day and there is also a carpark down Scarborough Avenue which is, roughly a 3 minute walk. Please allow time to get parked and walk to the church. The Reception has its own private carpark.

ATTIRE

We are kindly requesting a 'smart formal' attire to attend the church and reception. However we kindly ask that you avoid navy or champagne as these are the reserved colours for the wedding party.


CHILDREN

Having three gorgeous children between us means that our wedding will be child friendly. Please note if you are a parent your invitation will include any children you have. We will be having a disco, a bouncy castle and a secure private garden at the Reception so children can let off some steam. But we kindly ask you that children are not left unattended.


Staying over

If you are choosing to stay over, The Vine Hotel has kindly allocated a select number of rooms for our guests and honoured them a reduced rate. You can ring The Vine Hotel and book for our wedding weekend by stating you're a wedding guest. There are also quite a few cheap B&B's located within a short walk from the reception, as well as a Premier Inn located on the sea front.

The Church is located 1.3 miles away from the Vine Hotel so, you will need to travel to get to the reception. Most of you will be driving anyway but, we have included local taxi numbers just in case. Also the directions if you are driving and not familiar with the area.
