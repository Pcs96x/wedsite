OUR STORY

Gemmas version

It all started with 'I want to, lick your face!' No, it is not what it sounds like, but it is a funny story. During the first, of many lock downs Phill had shared a post on facebook that said 'I want to...' then people had to autofill the next three words with whatever their phone suggested. Well, of course for some unknown reason, my autofill was 'lick your face' so I posted it and we got talking. Me being stubbron old me waited for him to message me first but when he didn't, I took matters into my own hands and asked him out on a date! The first time we met in person, wasn't plain sailing either, I ran my mums car that low on fuel I ended up breaking down about 20 yards from his. I would like to say Phill came to the rescue and fixed the car and we had a fantstic date...but we didn't. We met, we spoke for about 5 mins and that was it. Surely the quickest date ever? Enough of the jokes (although that really is the truth), I knew from that short space of time, how well we would get on. After around 6 months of dating, Phill stuck a post-it note in the back of my phone case asking if I would be his girlfriend and the proposal was just as sweet... A trip down to the beach with our girls, all together as a family and he gets down on one knee in the rain and asks me to marry him. It was the easiest YES I have ever said.
